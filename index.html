About Me

Hello! I’m Udit Kumar Tiwari, a passionate Web Developer and Front-End Developer currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. My journey into the world of web development began during my school days when I often found myself fascinated by how information could be instantly accessed by simply searching the web. This curiosity led me to choose computer science in 11th standard, where I learned my first programming language, Python, and later ventured into front-end development.

Throughout my learning journey, I’ve created projects such as a calculator and a rock-paper-scissors game, gaining hands-on experience by following tutorials. My strong foundation in Python and C language has helped me approach web development with a solid understanding of coding principles.

I thrive on applying creative methodologies to problem-solving and enjoy collaborating with others to bring innovative ideas to life. I constantly seek to improve my skills by taking courses and exploring new resources to stay updated with the latest trends in the industry.

My ultimate goal is to build complete, professional websites that deliver both functionality and a great user experience. I’m always looking for opportunities to contribute my skills and collaborate on exciting web development projects.
